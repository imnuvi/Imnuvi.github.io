Select stuff the lazy way (in edit mode)

The most annoying thing i had to do in blender was selecting every single edge or corner by myself. Not a hard thing when wwhat you’re designing is small but when its got like 4 subdivision modifiers stuff becomes hard. So, to select rows of edges or corners that are not even linked but are on the same line, use ctrl + alt + left click To select alternates from anything you’ve got selected go to the select panel and click checker deselct
